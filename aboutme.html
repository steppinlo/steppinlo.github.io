About Me

I am a Bay Area native currently residing in NYC and attending Dev Bootcamp at their NYC campus. Prior to DBC, I attended CSU East Bay and graduated with a BA in Economics. I then moved out to NYC and was a technical recruiter, placing top technologists at various startups/banks/hedge funds/etc. During my tenure at CSUEB, I took a few programming courses (learning C++) and was a few courses short of completing a minor in CS.

Strangely enough, it was during my job as a recruiter that I realized my passion for engineering. Talking to developers on a daily basis, ranging from front-end developers all the way up to data scientists and CTOs, made me realized that I too wanted to be in that field. I have always been passionate about technology and being able to combine my fascination of technology to my career would be the ultimate dream.

Other things about me include avid traveler and total sports nut. I've been to well over 20 countries and it is an ambition of mine to hit up all 7 continents (3 more to go!). As a San Francisco native, I am a die-hard Giants, Niners, Warriors, and Sharks fan.
